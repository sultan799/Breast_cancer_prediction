Breast Cancer Detection App

Breast cancer is the most common invasive cancer in women and the second leading cause of cancer death in women after lung cancer.
A tumor is a mass or lump of tissue that may resemble swelling. Not all tumors are cancerous, but it is a good idea to see a doctor if one appears.
It is not always clear how a tumor will act in the future. Some benign tumors can become premalignant and then malignant. For this reason, it is best to monitor any growth.
A doctor often diagnoses breast cancer as the result of routine screening or when a woman approaches her doctor after detecting symptoms. Several diagnostic tests and procedures help to confirm a diagnosis.

This web app detects which type of cancer you are suffering from.
